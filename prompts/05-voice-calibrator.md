# Prompt 05 — Voice Calibrator

**What it does:** Builds a reusable brand voice brief from examples of your own writing so every AI output actually sounds like you — not a generic content machine.

**Run this once. Save the output. Paste it into every other prompt.**

**Best for:** Anyone who's tried AI content tools and thought "this doesn't sound like me at all."

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a brand voice analyst and writing coach.

I'm going to give you 3–5 examples of my own writing — social posts, emails, whatever I've actually published or sent. Your job is to study them and build me a reusable voice brief that I can paste into any AI prompt to make future outputs sound like me.

Here are my writing examples:

---
[PASTE EXAMPLE 1 HERE]
---
[PASTE EXAMPLE 2 HERE]
---
[PASTE EXAMPLE 3 HERE]
---
[PASTE MORE IF YOU HAVE THEM]
---

Based on these examples, give me a Voice Brief with the following sections:

**1. Voice in 3 words**
The 3 adjectives that best capture how I write.

**2. Sentence structure**
How do I write sentences? Short and punchy? Long and layered? Mix of both? Do I use fragments intentionally?

**3. Vocabulary patterns**
What words or phrases do I reach for? What do I never say? (List specific examples from my writing.)

**4. Emotional register**
What emotions does my writing carry? What does it feel like to read it?

**5. What I never sound like**
The 5 things I should tell every AI to avoid when writing in my voice.

**6. My signature moves**
Any recurring patterns, structural tics, or stylistic choices that make my writing recognizable.

**7. Ready-to-paste voice instruction**
A single paragraph I can copy and drop into any AI prompt to get output in my voice. Make it specific, not vague.
```

---

## How to Use the Output

Once you have your voice brief, add this to the top of any FLOWSTATE prompt:

> **My voice:** [paste the "Ready-to-paste voice instruction" paragraph here]

This single addition will make every output dramatically more you.

---

## Tips

- Use real examples, not idealized ones. Your most authentic writing — even if it's just a DM or a rough caption — is the best training data.
- Run this every 6 months. Your voice evolves.
- If the brief doesn't feel right, reply: `That doesn't capture me. Here's what's missing: [describe it]`
