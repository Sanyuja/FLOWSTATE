# Prompt 03: One Piece → 10 Formats

**Use this when:** You've already created something — a blog post, a podcast episode, a LinkedIn post, a talk — and want to squeeze every drop of value from it.

**Works with:** Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any AI assistant.

---

## The Prompt

Copy everything below the line, then paste your original content at the bottom.

---

You are a content repurposing strategist. Your job is to take one piece of existing content and transform it into 10 different formats without it feeling repetitive or copy-pasted.

Each format should feel native to its platform — a tweet should feel like a tweet, not a blog paragraph cut short. A reel script should feel like someone talking, not someone reading.

The creator's voice is: warm, direct, real. She doesn't use corporate language or fake hype. She speaks to women who are smart and busy.

Here is the original content:

[PASTE YOUR CONTENT HERE]

Please give me repurposed versions in these 10 formats:

1. **Instagram Reel Script** (60 sec, hook + value + CTA)
2. **Instagram Carousel** (5-slide outline: slide titles + one key point each)
3. **LinkedIn Post** (story-led, ends with a question)
4. **Twitter/X Thread** (5–7 tweets)
5. **Short Tweet** (single punchy insight from the piece, under 280 chars)
6. **Email Newsletter Intro** (first 3 paragraphs to hook readers into reading more)
7. **Pinterest Pin Description** (keyword-rich, 150 words)
8. **YouTube Short / TikTok Script** (30–45 sec, fast-paced)
9. **Podcast Episode Intro** (60 sec, spoken word style)
10. **Quotable Pull Quote** (one sentence that could become a graphic — bold, shareable)

---

## Tips

- Works best with content that has a clear point of view, not just information
- The carousel and thread formats often become the highest-performing pieces
- Save your pull quote — it makes a great cover image for your Medium post or LinkedIn header
