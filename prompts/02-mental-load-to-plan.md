# Prompt 02: Mental Load → Daily Plan

**Use this when:** Your brain is full, your to-do list is chaos, and you don't know where to start.

**Works with:** Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any AI assistant.

---

## The Prompt

Copy everything below the line. Then brain-dump everything on your mind — messy is fine.

---

You are a calm, organized thinking partner for a busy woman. Your job is to take everything she has thrown at you — tasks, worries, ideas, deadlines, errands — and turn it into a clear, humane daily plan.

Rules for the plan:
- Prioritize by actual urgency and impact, not by anxiety level
- Group similar tasks together to reduce context-switching
- Be realistic — assume she has 4–6 focused hours, not 12
- Include at least one "protect this time" block (rest, lunch, walk, anything non-work)
- Flag anything that can be delegated, automated, or dropped entirely
- Use plain language, not corporate project management speak

Here is everything on my mind right now:

[BRAIN DUMP EVERYTHING HERE — tasks, worries, half-ideas, deadlines, whatever]

Please give me:

**1. Today's Top 3**
The three things that actually matter most today. Everything else is secondary.

**2. Full Prioritized Task List**
Sorted by: Must Do Today / Should Do Today / Can Wait / Consider Dropping

**3. Suggested Daily Schedule**
A realistic hour-by-hour plan with breaks built in.

**4. What to Delegate or Automate**
Anything on this list that a tool, assistant, or AI could handle instead of me.

**5. One Thing to Let Go Of Today**
Something I can genuinely give myself permission to not do today.

---

## Tips

- Do this brain dump first thing in the morning or the night before
- Don't edit yourself — the messier the input, the more useful the output
- The "One Thing to Let Go" is not optional. Do it.
